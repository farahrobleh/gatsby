OUR STORY

HOW DID THIS HAPPEN?

Gatsby's Candy Factory was initially a collaborative interactive exhibit that debuted in San Francisco in August 2017. What was intended as a month-long run unexpectedly flourished as a celebration of candy and creativity that lasted for another eight sold-out months. In August 2018, a whole new pop-up came to New York City in SoHo’s Hudson Square neighborhood. So... ya.

A WONDERLAND OF FUN

WHAT'S ON DISPLAY?

In 20,000 square feet, we feature brand-new participatory installations of candy we’ve collected from around the world - sweets that invite curiosity, discovery and play. Some of our favorite chefs, chocolatiers, designers and artists have teamed up with us to tell their unique candy stories and engage all of your senses in unexpected ways. We hope that this museum (of sorts) will connect you to the delicious moments around New York and in your own daily life.
